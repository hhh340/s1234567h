The Archive Edit

Why Second Story

Considered pieces, honestly sourced.

We hand-select every piece from estate sales, mill closeouts, and private archives — nothing arrives here by accident.

Authenticated & Graded

Every luxury or designer piece is checked for authenticity and hand-graded for condition before it ever reaches the rail.

Slow Fashion, On Purpose

We buy less, choose carefully, and restore what's worth restoring. No overproduction, no landfill-bound deadstock.

Give Every Piece A Second Story

A portion of every sale funds textile recycling programs and local community clothing drives across Portland.
